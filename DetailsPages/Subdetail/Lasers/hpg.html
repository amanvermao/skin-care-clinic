Understanding Hyperpigmentation

Hyperpigmentation occurs when patches of skin become darker than the surrounding areas due to excess melanin production. This common condition can affect individuals of all skin types, with darker skin tones being particularly susceptible. Causes of hyperpigmentation include sun exposure, trauma to the skin, acne, and hormonal changes.

Types of Hyperpigmentation

Hyperpigmentation manifests in various forms, including:
  • Age spots
  • Freckles and lentigens
  • Uneven skin tone
  • Melasma
  • Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation

Effective Treatment Options

At Dr. Ujjvala Jadhav's Skin Clinic, we offer both medical and cosmetic treatments to address hyperpigmentation:
Medical Line of Treatment: Our medical treatments involve the application of specialized skin lightening creams that target excess melanin production and promote even skin tone.
Cosmetic Procedures: We also offer advanced cosmetic procedures to lighten areas of hyperpigmentation and rejuvenate the skin.
Laser Therapy: Utilizing Q-switched Nd:YAG Laser technology, our laser therapy treatments target and break down excess melanin, resulting in a clearer, more even complexion.
Chemical Peels: We offer a variety of chemical peels, including glycolic peels and Yellow peel, to exfoliate the skin and promote cellular turnover, reducing the appearance of hyperpigmentation and revealing brighter, smoother skin.

Treatment Selection and Customization
